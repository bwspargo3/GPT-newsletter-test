Regulators clear way to rewrite annuity illustration rules
InsuranceNewsNet  ·  2026-07-14
• The National Association of Insurance Commissioners' Life Insurance and Annuities Illustrations Working Group voted to reopen the annuity disclosure model to revise Model Regulation 245. • Regulators reviewed illustrations from 25 to 30 top annuity leaders, finding that two-thirds showed returns above 10%, with some as high as 27%. • The working group, led by Chairman Ben Slutsker, will consider revisions to illustration length, disclosure requirements, and presentation of non-guaranteed crediting rates.
Why it matters
This development directly impacts L&A consulting actuaries involved in product development and modeling, as revised illustration rules may affect how insurers present and disclose annuity products to consumers. The changes may also have implications for reserving and GAAP reporting, as insurers may need to reassess their assumptions and methodologies for annuity products.
